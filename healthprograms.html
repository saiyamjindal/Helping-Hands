Monthly Camps in Rural Areas

Helping hand india NGO will provide regular primary health services to the rural poor population in the remote areas through Mobile Medical Units (MMUs) and through Mobile Medical Vans (MMVs). There are many areas where the health facilities such as Govt, medical Centers or private health care facilities are not adequately available. We will conduct medical camps in far-flung rural areas and urban slums to cater to health needs of the under-served populations.
